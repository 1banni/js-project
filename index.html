By Will Bannister

Hover duel is a 2-player demolition game where you drive around, pick up ammo (orange bars) and health (hearts), and launch projectiles at your opponent. Last player standing wins.

Driving controls work like a car, with gas, brake/reverse, and turning. The map has two levels; the upper level can be accessed by entering any of the 4 platforms. These platforms are connected by bridges which can be driven over/under.

Hover Duel is intended to be reminiscent of Mario Kart Battle Mode, where you pop balloons of opponents to win.

Find a friend to play with you! It's no fun alone.
